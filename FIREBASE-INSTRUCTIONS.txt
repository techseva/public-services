================================================================
   STEP-BY-STEP FIREBASE SETUP GUIDE
================================================================

1. LOG IN
   - Go to: https://console.firebase.google.com/
   - Sign in with your Google Account.

2. CREATE PROJECT
   - Click the big box that says "Create a project" (or "Add project").
   - Enter a name: "AP-Digital-Services" (or anything you like).
   - Click "Continue".
   - Disable "Google Analytics" (toggle the switch off), then click "Create project".
   - Wait for it to finish, then click "Continue".

3. ADD APP (The important part!)
   - You will see 3 circular white icons: iOS, Android, and Web (</>).
   - Click the "Web" icon that looks like this: </>
   - App nickname: Enter "My Website".
   - Leave "Firebase Hosting" UNCHECKED.
   - Click "Register app".

4. COPY THE CODE
   - You will see a block of code under "Use npm".
   - Look for the part that starts with "const firebaseConfig = {".
   - Highlight and COPY everything from "const firebaseConfig = {" down to "};".
   - It looks like this:
     const firebaseConfig = {
       apiKey: "AIzaSy...",
       authDomain: "...",
       ...
     };

5. PASTE INTO SETUP TOOL
   - Open the "setup.html" file in your folder.
   - Paste the code you just copied into the big box.
   - Click "SAVE & CONNECT".

6. ENABLE DATABASE (Critical Final Step!)
   - Go back to the Firebase Console website.
   - On the left menu, click "Build" -> "Realtime Database".
   - Click "Create Database".
   - Choose a location (Singapore or US is fine), click "Next".
   - Choose "Start in Test Mode" (Select the second option).
   - Click "Enable".

DONE! Your website is now live and syncing.
